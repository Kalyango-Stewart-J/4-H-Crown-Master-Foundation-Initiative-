Empowering the Next Generation in UGANDA

Focus on academic and social development in a caring atmosphere.

The 4-H Crown Master Foundation Initiative boosts Primary Education to many children who are orphaned in the community and provides orphans and vulnerable children with the skills, mentorship, and community they need to thrive.

Our Core Initiatives

We focus on holistic development through the proven 4-H methodology of learning by doing.

Agricultural Education

Hands-on farming and gardening programs that teach sustainability, biology, and the value of nurturing growth.

STEM Mentorship

Connecting children with mentors in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math to unlock future career paths.

Civic Engagement

Empowering young children to be active citizens through community service projects and leadership training.

Mentorship That Matters

Every child deserves a champion. Our mentorship program pairs youth with caring adults who provide guidance, stability, and encouragement. Through regular interaction, we build confidence and self-esteem that lasts a lifetime.

  • One-on-one guidance
  • Career exploration
  • Life skills development
  • Emotional support
