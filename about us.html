4-H CROWN MASTER FOUNDATION

The 4-H Crown Master Foundation is a worldwide effort to open a door of hope for people of all ages, training and equipping them to pursue their God-given purpose in life. The foundation continues to establish an array of effective educational services for both children and adults, ranging from reading programs, to trades and technology certifications, to higher education. The Crown Foundation team is a growing community of compassionate educators who are helping people every day, enabling them to discover what it means to live life by God's design. The foundation is advanced not only by dedicated workers, but also by contributors to the Crown Foundation which has been established to support ongoing development and expansion.

VISION

We aspire to create the highest standard of education through development of children who are resilient and articulate to contribute positively to their communities, and dedicated to creating a world where every child, regardless of their background, has the opportunity to lead, grow, and succeed.

MISSION

The mission of The Crown Foundation is to reduce the impact of illiterate persons by promoting awareness, social responsibility, and the overall well-being of children through increasing access to sustainable educational needs, community influence, and equipping orphaned children to pursue their God-given purpose in life.

PROJECT GOALS

  • To promote confidentiality and basic standards of behaviour in the community
  • To invest in education and related ventures while engaging the community
  • To provide high quality, appropriate, and competitive primary education to children
  • To promote and foster the spirit of unity among people regardless of ethnicity or religion
  • To provide affordable all-round education to produce versatile and adaptable persons
  • To encourage people to know their life status by testing for HIV/AIDS
  • To foster access to affordable, quality education for talented but indigent students, especially girls
  • To reinstate the culture of abstaining from sex among the youth
