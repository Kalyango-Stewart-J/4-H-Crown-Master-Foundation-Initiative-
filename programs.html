Our Programs

Diverse pathways for every child to discover their passion and potential.




Junior Master Gardener

Agriculture Nutrition Sustainability

Children learn the art and science of gardening, understanding food systems, nutrition, and environmental stewardship.

Tech Wizards

STEM Technology Future Skills

Bridging the digital divide by teaching coding, robotics, and digital literacy skills to prepare youth for the future workforce.

Civic Leadership Academy

Leadership Public Speaking Service

Developing the next generation of community leaders through public speaking, debate, and service learning projects.

Creative Arts & Expression

Arts Mental Health Creativity

Hands-on experiments and inquiry-based learning to spark curiosity about the natural world.
